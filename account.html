About Digital Marketing

What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing is the use of online platforms like social media, search engines, and websites to promote products and services. It helps businesses reach more customers faster and more effectively.

Why it matters: Businesses use digital marketing to grow online, attract customers, and increase sales.

Key Areas

• Social Media Marketing (Facebook, Instagram, TikTok)
• Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
• Email Marketing
• Online Advertising (Google Ads)
• Content Creation
